Cream Puffs

Looking for a fun dessert that is easy to make and is guaranteed to delight your guests?
Try considering cream puffs as your next creation in the kitchen!
This version will include a light pastry dough and filled with rich vanilla cream!

Ingredients

  • 2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs

Directions

Step 1

Mix vanilla instant pudding mix, cream and
milk. Cover and refrigerate to set.

Step 2

Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).

Step 3

In a large pot, bring water and butter to a rolling boil.

Step 4

Stir in flour and salt until the mixture forms a ball.

Step 5

Transfer the dough to a large bowl. Using a wooden spoon or stand mixer,
beat in the eggs on at a time, mixing well after each.

Step 6

Drop by tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheet, spacing them a couple of inches apart.

Step 7

Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven, until
golden brown. Centers should be dry and have a shell like exterior.

Step 8

When the shells are cool, either split and fill them with the
or use a pastry bag to pipe the pudding into shells.

Step9

Enjoy!
